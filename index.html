Hello, I’m Rachel.

I am a social design practitioner with a lifelong commitment to co-creating solutions for a more equitable, sustainable world.

Over my 8+ years of experience, I have collaborated with policymakers, researchers, NGOs, artists, grassroots movements, cooperatives and start-ups to address pressing global challenges. My work applies systems thinking, human-centred design, participatory methods, and strategic communication, with the goal of building the social and digital infrastructure that communities need to leverage sociopolitical change. Above all, I strive to prove that through creative design and systematic organization, we can make our own resources and our own path toward the world we want to live in.

My Approach

My methodology combines ethnographic research with participatory design workshops. I believe in:

  • Empowering marginalized voices
  • Systems thinking for complex challenges
  • Iterative prototyping with communities
  • Measuring social impact qualitatively
