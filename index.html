Hello, I’m Rachel.

I am a social design practitioner with a lifelong commitment to co-creating solutions for a more equitable, sustainable world.

Over my 8+ years of experience, I have collaborated with policymakers, researchers, NGOs, artists, grassroots movements, cooperatives and start-ups to address pressing global challenges. My work applies systems thinking, human-centred design, participatory methods, and strategic communication, with the goal of building the social and digital infrastructure that communities need to leverage sociopolitical change. I hold a Bachelor of Arts (Honours) in Environmental Studies from Yale-NUS College, as well as Master of Environmental Management from Yale School of Environment. My projects span ecosystem building, research and writing, creative communications, and community fundraising.

Above all, I strive to prove that through creative design and systematic organization, we can make our own resources and our own path toward the world we want to live in.
